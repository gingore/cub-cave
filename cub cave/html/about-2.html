About Cub Cave

Cub Cave is a family-run shop that started as a support for students and adults who wanted to look for an escape in life. We understand that things can start to get overwhelming, so we created a cafe to help customers de-stress while indulging in our delicious recipes made fresh daily. We believe great food should be accessible to all who seek it, and are committed to sharing that love with our community!


If you have any questions or comments, please visit our contact tab!
